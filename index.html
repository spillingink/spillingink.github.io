The history of spilling ink

Hiya friends!

I got the name spilling ink because first I tried to make my newsletters with ink, but it kept spilling. Eventually my mom got so fed up that she said I should make my newsletters with my laptop then print it out.

I started Spilling ink in 2024. Selling my newsletters once a month for a quarter. But once 2025 began I wanted to reach a new goal and start coding a website. Now look at me!

The inspiration came to me when I wanted to join the school newsletter but wanted to make changes I thought I could not do in the school newsletter. Wanting to make a more “me”, newsletter I created spilling ink. Now, here I am every day working on something new to show the world.
